WELCOME TO KERALA

GODS OWN COUNTRY

Kerala, a very famous state in India, recognized as the “God’s Own Country” is a marginal state. The state is adjoined by plenty of beaches and also incorporated by mountains of Western Ghats and permeated with backwaters. Kerala is established in the southwestern section of India. Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Andhra Pradesh are the neighboring states to Kerala. Trivandrum is the metropolis city of Kerala. Kerala is a beautiful vacationist destination drawing tourist by its intrinsic beauty. The additional chief centers of fascination in Kerala are the zoo, boat houses, and islands. Some of the prominent tourist stations in Kerala are Kumarakom, Wayanad, Thekkady, Alappae, Kovalam and many more.
